# Review

Review the blog post and visual package.

Approve only if:
- the draft is focused and not a broad recap
- claims are source-backed or clearly framed as uncertainty
- the post is public-safe
- a generated header image artifact exists, or an explicit image fallback/blocker is acceptable
- any inline SVG charts/diagrams are source-grounded, accessible, and actually clarify the article
- the Portal publish destination is clear

Routes:
- `approved`: continue to publish-and-attach
- `changesRequested`: return to revise
- `rejected`: close
