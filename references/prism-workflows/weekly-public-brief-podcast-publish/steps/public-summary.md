# Public Summary

## Outcome

Create a public-safe weekly brief that gives readers a small peek into guild operations, shows that RaidGuild is active and dynamic, and stays safely above the line on private detail.

## Use This Context

- resolved period intake artifacts
- relevant public-safe digests, artifacts, and notes from the chosen weekly window
- any human guidance about emphasis, tone, or exclusions

Use `prism-api-reader` when retrieval is needed.

## Durable Artifacts

Write both:
- `weekly-summary.md`
- `weekly-summary.json`

The JSON artifact should include:
- date window
- major public-safe themes
- visible activity or momentum signals
- governance or project movement that is safe to mention
- exclusions and confidence notes where needed
- recommended CTA language

## Positioning Rules

- optimize for a public-safe peek into guild operations
- show that RaidGuild is active, dynamic, and visibly working across projects, coordination, and governance
- do not include client names, private strategy, unpublished operational detail, or participant-sensitive context
- do not over-claim based on thin evidence
- prefer visible momentum, activity, and contribution patterns over internal specifics
- help a new reader understand why following along or joining the Portal would be worthwhile

## CTA Rule

Prepare a short call to action that points readers toward the Portal to:
- follow along
- see active projects
- join upcoming sessions
- plug into real opportunities across RaidGuild

The CTA should feel like a natural close, not ad spam.
