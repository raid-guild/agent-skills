# Steering Intake

Outcome: confirm the request has enough human steering to begin drafting.

This is a human gate, not an agent work step. Do not draft, research, generate media, or create Portal/Payload records from this step.

Approval means a human has added a steering comment that gives the topic, angle, audience, source material, constraints, and any publishing expectations clearly enough for the draft step to proceed.

If the request has no steering comment, or the steering is too vague to produce a useful one-off blog post, leave the workflow at intake and request clarification.

Routes:

- approved: continue to Draft.
- changesRequested: stay at Steering Intake until the missing steering is supplied.
- rejected: close the request.
