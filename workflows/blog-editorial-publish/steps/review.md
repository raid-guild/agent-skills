# Human Review

Human gate.

Reviewer should inspect:

- `strategy-brief.md`
- latest draft
- `media-plan.md`
- `voice-pass.md`
- `safety-result.json`
- source notes and unresolved questions

Approve only if:

- the angle is useful and bounded
- claims are source-backed
- voice fits the intended account/channel
- safety blockers are resolved or accepted as non-public
- media direction is acceptable
- Portal destination and visibility are clear

Routes:

- `approved`: continue to `portal-publish-prep`
- `changesRequested`: continue to `revise`
- `rejected`: close
