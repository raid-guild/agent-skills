# Voice Pass

Use `rg-brand-voice`.

Create `voice-pass.md` and update `draft.md` or create `draft-voiced.md`.

Choose the voice layer:

- RaidGuild core
- AI Solutions
- Queen Raida
- internal/community
- lore/story mode

Apply:

- concrete before mythic
- source-backed specificity
- anti-pattern cleanup
- channel-appropriate tone
- persona/account fit

For lore mode, preserve story texture while avoiding generic AI mysticism, copied external style, or unsupported factual claims.

Do not perform public safety review here; that happens in `safety-pass`.
