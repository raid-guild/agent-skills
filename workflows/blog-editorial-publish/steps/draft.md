# Draft

Use `strategy-brief.md` and available source artifacts.

Create `draft.md` with:

- title or working title
- slug suggestion
- excerpt/dek
- body
- source notes
- open questions or review risks

Rules:

- Keep claims grounded in available evidence.
- Preserve uncertainty when needed.
- Do not invent quotes, dates, people, project status, client claims, governance outcomes, or Portal facts.
- Use short quotes only when exact source wording is available; otherwise paraphrase.

Mode-specific guidance:

- `fireside`: one clear thesis, 600-900 words by default, no broad session recap.
- `lore`: write as story/lore, not newsletter; keep real-world claims clearly separated in source notes.
- `generic`/`steered`: match the steering brief and avoid generic thought leadership.
