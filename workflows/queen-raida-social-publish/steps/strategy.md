# Social Strategy

Use `rg-content-strategy` and `rg-brand-voice`.

Read `queen-raida-social-intake.md` and choose the social strategy. Keep the plan source-backed and useful before making it stylized.

Produce `queen-raida-social-strategy.md` with:

- target audience
- recommended format and action
- one primary angle
- optional secondary angle
- public CTA or destination, if any
- tone constraints
- Queen Raida texture level: `none`, `light`, or `campaign`
- selected classification, if useful: `GUILD DISPATCH`, `RAID REPORT`, `FIELD REPORT`, `ARCHIVE ENTRY`, `AGENT MANIFEST`, `LOW THAUMIC RISK`, `EXPERIMENTAL RITUAL`, `FORBIDDEN AUTOMATION`, `WARNING`, or `CONTAINMENT BREACH`
- rationale for using or skipping lore texture

For support, complaints, sensitive issues, operational incidents, or high-risk topics, use minimal or no lore texture and prioritize clarity.
