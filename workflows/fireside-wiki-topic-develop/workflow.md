# Fireside Wiki Topic Develop

This workflow creates or updates Portal wiki pages from a Fireside session topic, approved Field Notes topic map, or research seed.

Wiki pages are living maps, not marketing posts. The workflow decomposes topics before drafting, preserves open questions, and publishes only after review.

Use `rg-research` for source ledgers, current-signal checks, HN scouting, and claim confidence before drafting or publishing wiki content.
