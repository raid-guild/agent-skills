# Topic Map

Use `rg-content-strategy` and `rg-brand-voice`.

Read `fireside-source-pack.md` and create `fireside-topic-map.md` with three buckets:

1. Session-grounded notes or reports.
2. Broad wiki topic candidates for Portal wiki pages.
3. Narrow blog candidates with one clear thesis and tight scope.

Broad technology themes, further reading, latest news, research signals, future prompts, and evolving context belong in wiki candidates first. Mark a blog candidate only when the source pack supports a focused publishable angle.

Include routing recommendations for each candidate.
