# Content Pillars

Use these as planning buckets, not fixed slogans.

## Builders And Shipping

Work, artifacts, demos, repos, launches, experiments, technical progress.

## Coordination And Governance

DAO proposals, member systems, decision flow, accountability, participation, collective work.

## Agents And Automation

Prism, Queen Raida, workflow automation, AI-assisted operations, human review, source discipline.

## Culture And Lore

Origin stories, guild identity, Moloch, weird builder culture, mythic texture, community memory.

## Client And Offer Clarity

AI Solutions, service offers, workflow transformation, consulting, implementation, proof, buyer education.

## Knowledge And Memory

Portal, wikis, briefings, digests, archives, session notes, reusable learning.

## Rule

Every piece should usually have one primary pillar and, at most, one secondary pillar. Too many pillars means the angle is not sharp enough.
