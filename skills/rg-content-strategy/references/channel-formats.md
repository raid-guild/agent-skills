# Channel Formats

Use this to choose a content shape before drafting.

## Short Social

Best for:

- one insight
- one receipt
- one launch/update
- one reply
- one clear ask

Structure:

1. concrete signal
2. meaning or implication
3. optional CTA or receipt

## Discord

Best for:

- coordination
- review asks
- internal/community updates
- publishing summaries

Structure:

1. what happened
2. what is needed
3. owner or destination
4. deadline or next step if relevant

## Blog Or Field Note

Best for:

- explaining a pattern
- preserving a story
- turning a session into durable knowledge
- showing work and implications

Structure:

1. thesis or scene
2. source-backed sections
3. concrete example
4. implication
5. next step or open question

## Newsletter Or Brief

Best for:

- periodic synthesis
- multiple signals
- recent community activity

Structure:

1. top signal
2. grouped updates
3. why it matters
4. links or receipts
5. next actions

## Website Or Sales Page

Best for:

- offers
- positioning
- conversion
- service explanation

Structure:

1. audience problem
2. offer
3. proof or method
4. boundaries
5. CTA
