# Current Signal Ranking

Use this when scanning recent public sources for ideas, risks, or commentary angles.

## Score Dimensions

- `relevance`: connection to RaidGuild, agents, coordination, dev tools, governance, publishing, CMS, security, or client-relevant operations.
- `freshness`: how recent and still-active the item is.
- `sourceQuality`: primary source, reputable outlet, direct project source, or high-signal community discussion.
- `actionability`: whether the signal can inform a decision, brief, response, wiki page, or content angle.
- `risk`: legal, financial, privacy, security, political, personal, or reputational sensitivity.
- `novelty`: whether the item adds something beyond generic trend chatter.

## Output Shape

For each candidate:

- title
- source URL
- discussion URL when relevant
- date
- why it matters
- RaidGuild/agent/workflow connection
- risk notes
- recommended use: ignore, watch, research deeper, content seed, wiki seed, operator alert

Prefer 3-7 ranked candidates unless the operator asks for a broader scan.
